Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking Liquid Arts

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance for standard events, and 8+ weeks for bespoke menu development.

Do you provide the alcohol?

Yes, we offer full beverage sourcing at wholesale pricing. Corkage options are also available.

Do you travel outside of LA?

Absolutely. We serve all of California, nationwide destinations, and international events.

Can I book a mixology class?

Yes! We offer private and corporate mixology masterclasses. Contact us for pricing.
